About Phil Wears

Phil Wears was founded in 2025 with a mission to bring bold, urban designs to the custom clothing industry. We believe that what you wear is an extension of who you are, and we're here to help you make that statement loud and clear.

Our team of talented designers and branding experts work closely with each client to create unique, eye-catching designs that represent their vision and style.

Whether you're an individual looking to express yourself or a business seeking to create branded merchandise, Phil Wears is your partner in making a lasting impression.
